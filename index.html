About Me

My name is Jabili Gadde, and I am an incoming junior Computer Science major and Interactive Multimedia minor attending The College of New Jersey. I took my first programming class when I was a sophomore in high school. I absolutely loved it, so I continued taking computer science classes for the rest of high school, and decided to pursue my passion by majoring in computer science in college.

Since then, I have taken the time to indulge in my interest by learning subtopics of Computer Science, such as Data Science and Mobile App Development, as well as attending hackathons and learning other programming languages in my free time like Python and Swift, and interning at American Express as a Software Engineer Intern, working on Rasa bots. I also decided to share my love for technology by working as a Java tutor, working as an Assistant Instructor for technology related courses over the summer, as well as becoming a peer tutor at my college. I hope to become a Software Engineer or Software Developer after college so that I can contribute to this amazing field.
